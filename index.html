Our Story

SPOTIFRIES was born from a simple passion: creating the perfect french fry. What started as a food truck in 2023 has grown into a sensation that's changing how people experience this beloved side dish.

Our secret? We source only the finest potatoes, cut them fresh daily, and cook them to crispy perfection using our signature double-frying technique. Then comes the magic – our innovative flavor combinations that will have you coming back for more.

At SPOTIFRIES, we believe that french fries aren't just a side dish – they're the main event. That's why we put so much care into every batch, ensuring that each customer experiences fry perfection with every bite.
